
## Objective

Several [theories and research fields](../quality-criteria/principles-reasoning-argumentation-by-field.md) establish the principles of validity, reasoning, explanations, understanding versus logical validity. The current classification moves from a **field-by-field inventory** to a **cross-domain taxonomy**.
